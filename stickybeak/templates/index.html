Who can benefit from using the app?

The confused one

Perhaps you are not the most comfortable with technology, and you do not yet have the idea how to verify information.
You are not sure if the article you just read is real or not.

The curious one

While you do fact check sometimes, you feel like browsing through different articles eats up too much time, so you want a quick and handy way to check things on the go.

The collector

While you may not fall for the fake news yourself, you still like reading about them, whether it be because they are entertaining, or you want to inform others.
